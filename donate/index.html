Donate

We need you as much as Louisville needs us.

When you make a donation to New Directions, you help elderly and disabled homeowners stay in their homes. You help fund youth programs and after-school childcare. You help the residents of our housing programs. You help improve neighborhoods all across Louisville and Southern Indiana. Most important, you help ensure a brighter future for your fellow citizens.

Ways to give

New Directions makes it easy to donate, whether it’s a one-time
donation by mail or a recurring payment with credit card.

Donate by Mail 
Gifts may be sent through the mail to:

New Directions Housing Corporation
1617 Maple Street
Louisville, KY 40210

If you would like to designate your gift to a specific
New Directions program, please indicate this on your check.

Thanks to our partners in caring.

New Directions is indebted to our generous supporters for nearly 50 years of helping us achieve our mission. Without them, we couldn’t have placed over 900 families in affordable housing, repaired countless homes for the elderly and disabled, or provided other resources to families in need across Louisville and Southern Indiana. With them at our side, we look forward to continuing to fulfill our promise of stability and security for our neighbors and community.
