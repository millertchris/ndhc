Early childhood education

We believe every child deserves a head start.

The St. Benedict Center for Early Childhood Education is one of the few childcare centers in West Louisville to offer Head Start and Early Head Start curriculum to children in low-income families. Children from six weeks to six years old are eligible for year-round, engaging and age-appropriate curriculum that’s offered through a partnership with Greater Louisville Headstart and Community Coordinated Child Care (4-C).

Those interested in enrolling should contact St. Benedict’s family advocate (502-772-7324), who will help you through the registration process. Enrollment is contingent on availability.
